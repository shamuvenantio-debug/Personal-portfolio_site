About Me

Hi, I’m Venantio Kudakwashe, a recent graduate in Information Technology from Chinhoyi University of Technology. I’m passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems and create meaningful impact. Over the past few years, I’ve built a strong foundation in software development, data science, and analytics, earning industry-recognized certifications from IBM, HP, and Dataquest in areas such as Python programming, SQL, and data analysis. My academic journey was capped by a dissertation project where I developed an online tutoring system to connect underprivileged students with volunteer tutors, combining my technical skills with a mission-driven purpose. I enjoy working on web development projects, exploring modern frameworks, and continuously expanding my knowledge in cloud technologies, databases, and full-stack development. My strength lies in being adaptable — whether it’s coding, analyzing data, or designing systems, I’m always eager to learn and apply new tools to deliver effective solutions. Looking ahead, I’m focused on building a career where I can contribute to innovative IT projects, sharpen my skills, and grow into a professional who bridges technical expertise with creative problem-solving.
